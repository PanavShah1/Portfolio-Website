Speed Cubing

If you catch me caught up in the intricate dance of cubes, you've stumbled upon one of my greatest fascinations - cubing. The art of solving these puzzles has become more than just a hobby. It has become a thrilling journey filled with twists and turns.

What is Speed Cubing

Chances are you have stumbled upon one of those rusty, old, classic rubiks cubes. Well believe it or not, we have made a literal sport out of that. Solving various types of cubes in the record time is the goal of this unique sport. It goes beyong the 3x3's that you know of, we solve all types of cubes, including pyramids, dodecahedrons.

Me in cubing

I was introduced to the fascinating realm of cubing by my dad. He taught me how to solve one side of the 3x3 when I was not even 7. And now I can solve all sorts of cubes. You won't find me go by a day without touching my cubes.
